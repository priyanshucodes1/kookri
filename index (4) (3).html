Our Story

Born from Tibetan Heart

Nestled in the heart of Dum Dum, Kolkata, Kookri Café brings you the authentic taste of Tibet — bold, honest, and full of warmth. Every dish is crafted with recipes passed down through generations.

From our signature Thukpa soups to crackling dry starters and hearty gravies, every bite is an experience of the Himalayan spirit.
